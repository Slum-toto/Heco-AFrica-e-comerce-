HECO-Africa Slum Tour

Join our immersive journey through Kibera and other communities. Experience firsthand the challenges, resilience, and opportunities in urban settlements while contributing to sustainable change.

Tour Highlights

  • Guided walk through Kibera community
  • Visit to local entrepreneurship projects
  • Interaction with community leaders and youth
  • Visit to HECO-Africa training centers
  • Cultural exchange and traditional meals
  • Photography opportunities (with permission)
  • Roundtable discussion on community solutions

Tour Schedule

Duration

4-6 Hours

Group Size

2-15 People

Contribution

From $25-$50 per person

All contributions directly support HECO-Africa community programs

Important Information

  • Wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Bring a reusable water bottle
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Photography restrictions in certain areas
  • Safety briefing provided before tour
  • All ages welcome (minors with guardian)

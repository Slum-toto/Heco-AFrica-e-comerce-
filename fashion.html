Our Fashion Academy

We bridge the gap between talent and opportunity by providing comprehensive fashion education to underprivileged youth.

Professional Training

6-month intensive program covering design principles, pattern making, sewing techniques, and textile knowledge. Hands-on training with industry-standard equipment.

Entrepreneurship

Business skills training including marketing, pricing, branding, and financial management. Graduates receive startup kits to launch their own fashion businesses.

Mentorship Program

Each student is paired with an industry professional who provides guidance, networking opportunities, and career advice throughout their journey.

Voices of Our Young Men

Breaking stereotypes and building futures. Hear from the young men who are reshaping African fashion.

Brian Omondi

Fashion Graduate, 2023

"People told me fashion was for women, but HECO-Africa showed me that creativity has no gender. I now run a successful menswear line that challenges stereotypes. Thank you for seeing my potential when no one else did."

David Kamau

Tailoring Specialist, 2022

A Message From Our Founder

"At HECO-Africa, we believe that education is the most powerful weapon to change the world. But for many children in our communities, even the most basic school requirements are out of reach. Every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and dream of a better future.

Your sponsorship doesn't just pay for books and uniforms—it gives hope, restores dignity, and opens doors to opportunities these children never thought possible. Together, we can ensure that no child is left behind because of circumstances beyond their control."

Owino Charles Okoth
Founder & CEO, HECO-Africa
